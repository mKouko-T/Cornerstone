# Cornerstone Genesis: Handoff Package

This artifact formally bridges the Foundation Zero design phase into the Cornerstone execution phase. This chat serves as the frozen design record of the methodology. Execution belongs in the new Genesis workspace.

## 1. Mission
Build the **Canonical Real Estate Development Operating Model (CREDOM)** from first principles, and implement its first realization in Cornerstone.

## 2. Accepted Assumptions
- **Foundation Zero Lite v1.0** is accepted as the starting hypothesis.
- The **methodology is frozen** pending empirical evidence.
- **Reality validates implementations, not ontology.** The business model must be derived mathematically and logically before it is tested against today's implementation.

## 3. Execution Sequence (The 11-Layer Genesis Process)
1. **Layer 0 — First Principles**: Truths every subsequent model must obey.
2. **Layer 1 — Universal Ontology**: Actor, Asset, Resource, Obligation, Agreement, Event, State, Transaction, Value, Evidence.
3. **Layer 2 — Domain Ontology**: Specialization to Real Estate (Customer, Project, Unit, Reservation).
4. **Layer 3 — Business Invariants**: Unbreakable rules.
5. **Layer 4 — Economic Engine**: The value transformation system.
6. **Layer 5 — Decisions**: Strategic choices defining behavior.
7. **Layer 6 — Processes**: Workflows.
8. **Layer 7 — Interfaces**: External boundaries.
9. **Layer 8 — Reality Validation**: Compare against current implementations to identify delta.
10. **Layer 9 — Implementation Ledger**: Prioritized engineering obligations.
11. **Layer 10 — Historical Witnesses**: Consult archives only for remaining gaps.

## 4. Success Criteria
- The business model perfectly explains the enterprise.
- Reality either validates the model or falsifies it with genuine business constraints.
- The software implementation measurably improves business outcomes.

## 5. Non-Goals
- **No more governance design.** The architecture phase is over.
- **No speculative architecture.** Every artifact must justify its existence.
- **No optimization before understanding.** We model the bedrock before we automate.
